MHAPh

Mental Health AWHEREness PH

In this crowdmapping project, we rely on volunteers' local knowledge (familiarity of the place where they are located) to locate the MH Services. See the MHAPh Map here.

  • If you are currently at the MH service, click the hand button on the left to focus the map on your current location.

  • You may also use the search button to look for the location. Then zoom in on the location of the MH service.

  • Once zoomed in, click the pin button on the left and drag it on the MH service's location. Enter place name then click "Get URL". Make sure to copy and save the resulting URL. You may then provide the details of the MH service and its location's URL using the plus button below.
